Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Supply Chain & Operations Management Area at Mitchell E. Daniels, Jr. School of Business, Purdue University, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Annabelle Feng, Prof. J. George Shanthikumar, and Prof. William B. Haskell. Before I joined Purdue, I earned B.Eng. in Industrial Engineering and Technology Management from The University of Hong Kong. My research focuses on decision-making under uncertainty and ambiguity in supply chain management.

I will be joining the Department of Decision Analytics and Operations at City University of Hong Kong as an Assistant Professor in Summer 2025.
