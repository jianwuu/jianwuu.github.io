Bio

I am an Assistant Professor of Management Sciences in the College of Business, City University of Hong Kong. Before joining CityU, I obtained my Ph.D. from Mitch Daniels School of Business at Purdue University, under the supervision of Prof. Qi Annabelle Feng, Prof. J. George Shanthikumar, and Prof. William B. Haskell. My research focuses on decision-making under uncertainty in supply chain management.
